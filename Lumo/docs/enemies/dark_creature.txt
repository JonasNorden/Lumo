Dark Creature – Design Specification

(Lumo Gameplay System)

Core Identity

Dark Creature is a stand-ground void entity that controls space through projectile hazards and interaction with light.

It does not patrol or chase the player.
It remains mostly stationary, drifting slightly to feel alive.

The creature becomes dangerous when the player enters its aggro radius.

Behavior Overview
Idle

Default state.

Behavior:

stationary

slight floating/drifting motion

no facing direction

no patrol

no chase

The creature exists as a dark hovering mass.

Aggro

When Lumo enters the aggro radius, the creature prepares to attack.

The aggro radius is defined by:

aggroTiles

in the level editor.

Aggro is purely distance-based.

Dark Spell Attack
Cast Charge

Before firing, Dark Creature performs a short charge.

Duration:

~0.5 seconds

During this time:

dark energy gathers around the creature

subtle particle effect appears

attack can be interrupted by Lumo’s aura

If Lumo’s aura reaches the creature during charge:

cast is cancelled
Projectile Behavior

The projectile is the Dark Spell.

Properties:

ballistic arc trajectory

gravity affected

aimed at Lumo’s position at cast time

no homing after launch

slight target jitter (~3px) to avoid robotic precision

If Lumo stands still, the projectile should hit.

Projectile Visuals

Flight sprite:

void_m_04

Sprite rotates using velocity direction:

angle = atan2(vy, vx)

This ensures the tail always points backward.

Impact

When Dark Spell hits:

ground

terrain

or Lumo

it enters the hazard state.

Impact animation sequence:

void_m_03
void_m_02
void_m_01
Hazard

After impact, a void hazard remains on the ground.

Duration:

5 seconds

Properties:

damages Lumo

causes pushback

drains Lumo energy

fades out after duration

Hazard always settles on the ground surface.

Combat Interactions
Lumo Aura

When Dark Creature is inside Lumos aura:

it cannot cast

it is not damaged

The aura neutralizes attacks but does not destroy the creature.

Flare

Flare light does not damage the creature.

Instead:

Dark Creature consumes flare light

flare lifetime drains faster while inside aggro radius

visual particles travel from flare → creature

Pulse

Dark Creature can only be killed by:

Lumo Pulse

Pulse deals HP damage.

Body Contact

Touching Dark Creature causes:

pushback

energy loss

This remains dangerous even when the creature is neutralized by aura.

Death

Dark Creature does not use death sprites.

Instead it dissolves.

Sequence:

creature begins fading

void particles spawn at bottom edge

particles rise upward like bubbling void energy

particles fade and shrink

creature disappears

Particles should:

rise vertically

move slowly

have minimal sideways drift

Level Design Role

Dark Creature is a space-control enemy.

It creates three gameplay zones:

Long range
projectile threat

Mid range
aura neutralizes casting

Close range
body contact danger

The player must balance:

positioning

light usage

pulse energy

Editor Support

Editor visualization includes:

Aggro radius:

single red dashed ring

Light sources (lantern etc):

yellow double ring

Trigger entities:

purple single ring

All overlays share the same global toggle.

Design Rules

Dark Creature must always remain:

stand-ground

readable

interruptible by light

killable only by Pulse

It should create tactical obstacles rather than chase pressure.
